Pricing

The Budget Plan

The budget plan will use low grade items, that includes everything from the floors to the furniture. This is one of the most popular plans for a young family. Items in this plan are cheaper to replace and it comes with a great warranty. Based on the size, this plan amount will stay close to the starting price.

Turns Heads

The Turn Heads plan will use medium grade items, that includes everything from the floors to the furniture. This is one of the most popular plans for young adults. Items in this plan tend to be designed for a younger audience. Lots of hip ideas. Based on the size, this plan amount will pass the starting price by 20%.

Breaks Necks

The Breaks Necks plan will use top of the line items, that includes everything from the floors, furniture, and the smallest of detail. This is one of the most popular plans for an older audience. Items in this plan are very expensive and unique. Items that are lost or broken in this plan will be very expensive to replace. This plan is designed for the wealthy elite class. Based on the size, this plan amount will pass the starting price by 50%.
