What We Do!

Having issues on designing your home? Do you ever feel ashamed when you host a party or have people over? Let us relight that homeowner confidence back up! Here at White Space Designs we work with some of the TOP art designers, architects, and engineers to help create the home of your dreams!

Here's how it works:

On the SERVICE page you will put together your ideas by going through our designer check list. This checklist is VERY important for your design. With the information that you provide will define the future of your design. So PLEASE do not skim through this page. Once all of the information is gathered, please visit our CONTACT page for final the instructions.

Before We Start....

Please have something ready like a notepad or device to write down any information that is requested. This information will later be used to create your design. Any type of media entries will be sent directly to one of our specialists listed in our CONTACT page. We suggest that you go through our checklist before any type of entry. Most importantly, should you need any further information, please do not hesitate to CONTACT us! One last thing, our team does not like to push ideas on to our customers, we just want to make what our customers want.

Why is this so important?

Our system is built to evaluate all different types of pictures, area sizes, likes or dislikes, and ideas. Once all of this information is in our system, the extracted data will then be applied to thousands of designs archived in our digital library for them to be viewed by you!
