Designer Check List Part 1

We Need Your Pictures!
If you could please provide us with any type of picture of either an area or area's that you would like for us to change. The pictures can be anything from an empty room to a full room that you found in a magazine.
We Would LOVE to hear your Ideas!
We live by one motto, there is no such thing as a bad idea! We believe in that the smallest ideas can make the BIGGEST changes!
How big or small is the area?
If you could please provide us with the length, width, and height of the area that will need to be redesigned. You can also include special
Do Wants
Please list features from colors to a famous artist that you would like to see incorporated into your design.
No NOT wants
Please list features of any type or items that you do NOT want incorporated into your design. This will help our "Build Engine" create ideas that will best suit your design.
Most Importantly... HAVE FUN!
These requirements might seem overbearing to some people but do not forget, our team here at WSD, will help you through this process step-by-step until YOU are satisfied.
