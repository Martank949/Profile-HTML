White Space Designs was created by a small group of designers from southern California. They got together one day and brilliantly came up with an idea on to create an A.I. system where thousands of different ideas can be thrown together and a computer can generate different designs based of the thousands of ideas. Here at White Space Designs, we put together a team consisting of fashion and interior designers, art & design students from the prestigious schools of LCAD and USC, and engineers from all around the world. Our team here at WSD works with the only A.I. system that is able to generate thousands of designs in a matter of seconds. With our system, you will be able to pick out specific features or down to the smallest details, to best fit your home.

White Space Designs Headquarters

This building is our main headquarters located in the beautiful sunny southern park of Baja, Mexico. It was actually designed by our founding four designers. The designers like to call this design "The Hive". It took roughly about 10 years to construct. If you would like to come and visit "The Hive" please contact our customer service department. We do not like to charge our visitors, it's simply a tour to get a free taste of the talent WSD holds.
